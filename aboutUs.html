  • Sleep and Health Clinic is a UK registered medical services company, that specialises in providing top quality care for patients with sleep disorders. We offer diagnosis and treatments for all sleep disorders.

MEET THE TEAM

Our partners are experts in their field with a vast experience in managing sleep disorders.
Dr McNeillis has vast experience in the management of sleep disorders. She is based at University College Hospitals. She currently practices in the NHS and private sector. Her anaesthesia skills give her a unique perspective on the function of the upper airway under anaesthesia and natural sleep.

DR SARA MCNEILLIS
